When HandTrackerMode is active the player controls a hand cursor in the screen so in order to rotate the cube the player needs to make a fist and move it to the edges of the screen to orient the cube.
To move a side the player needs to either get the cursor on a button and close their hand leaving only the index finger up or press the key controlling the side(R, O, B, G, Y, W).
When HandTrackerMode isn't activated the cube's is controlled with RMB.
To move a side the player now needs to either click on the corresponding button or press the key controlling the side(R, O, B, G, Y, W).
If you want to reset the cube you can press the restart button.
If you want to scramble the cube you can press th scramble button.
